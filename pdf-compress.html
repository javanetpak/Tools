Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF compressor free to use?

Yes, our PDF compression tool is completely free with no hidden charges.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress PDF files up to 50MB each. For larger files, consider splitting them first.

Will my PDF quality be affected?

The quality depends on your selected compression level. Higher compression may reduce image quality.
