How to Use Our Image Compressor

Follow these simple steps to optimize your images:

  1. Drag and drop your images into the upload area or click to browse your files
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values mean better quality)
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep the original format)
  4. Optionally resize your images to specific dimensions
  5. Click "Compress Images" and wait for the processing to complete
  6. Download your optimized images individually or all at once

Why Compress Your Images?

Image optimization offers several benefits:

  • Faster website loading: Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Better SEO: Page speed is a ranking factor for search engines
  • Reduced bandwidth usage: Save on hosting costs and data transfer
  • Improved storage efficiency: Store more images in less space
  • Social media ready: Optimized images look great on all platforms
